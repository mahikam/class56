How to make Pecan Brownies

Ingredients Needed For the Brownie: 1/2 cup of melted butter, 1 cup of sugar,1 teaspoon of vanilla extract, 2 eggs, 1/2 cup of all purpose flour, 1/2 cup of Hershey's Cocoa, 1/4 teaspoon baking powder, 1/4 teaspoon of salt, 1/2 a cup of chopped/crushed pecans

1. Heat your oven to 350Â°F and lightly grease/butter an 8 inch square baking pan.

2. Mix butter, sugar, and vanilla together in a large bowl. Once done add eggs and stir well. In another bowl mix flour, cocoa, baking powder, and salt, once done slowly add the mixture to the large bowl until blended. Stir in the pecans and spread into the prepared pan.

3. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until the brownie mixture doesn't stick to the sides of the pan. Add the Chocolate forsting (optional) and enjoy once cooked.

Ingredients Needed For the Frosting(Optional): 1 and 1/3 cups of powdered sugar, 2 tablespoons of Hershey's Cocoa, 3 tablespoons of butter, 2 tablespoons of milk, 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1/4 cup of chopped/crushed pecans
